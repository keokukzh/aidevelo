# Initial Concept
Aidevelo is a control plane for autonomous AI companies, designed to orchestrate multiple organizations, their org charts, goals, and agent-driven workflows.

# Product Vision
Aidevelo aims to be the definitive "Operating System" for the next generation of autonomous organizations. It provides the high-level orchestration layer that connects strategic business goals to the low-level execution of diverse AI agents, regardless of their underlying technology or runtime.

# Target Audience
Aidevelo is built for:
- **Individual Entrepreneurs:** Building "one-person unicorns" by leveraging teams of autonomous AI agents.
- **Agencies:** Managing complex, multi-agent AI teams for a diverse portfolio of clients.
- **Enterprise Teams:** Orchestrating internal AI workflows and automating business processes at scale.

# Core Goals
- **5-Minute Magic:** A fresh user should go from installation to seeing their first AI-driven task completion in under five minutes.
- **Board-Level Abstraction:** The primary interface focuses on what matters to a business leader: goals, budgets, approvals, and strategic progress, rather than raw technical logs.
- **Agent Agnostic:** Aidevelo is unopinionated about agent runtimes, supporting everything from local scripts and Claude Code to external OpenClaw hooks.

# Key Features
- **Hierarchical Task Management:** Every single action taken by an agent must trace back to a primary company goal through a clear parentage of tasks.
- **Autonomous Org Charts:** Define your CEO, executives, and reporting lines. Each "employee" is an AI agent with specific roles and adapter configurations.
- **Financial Orchestration:** Integrated tracking of revenue and expenses at the company level, providing a real-time view of the organization's health.

# User Experience Principles
- **Summaries First:** The default view provides human-readable summaries of intent and progress.
- **Progressive Disclosure:** Deep technical details (logs, transcripts) are available for expert troubleshooting but remain hidden behind a clean, high-level interface.
- **Work-Centric Communication:** Interaction with agents happens within the context of tasks, goals, and approvals, ensuring all "chat" remains focused on achieving company objectives.

# Long-Term Impact
Aidevelo serves as the catalyst for a new era of organizational efficiency, enabling the creation and management of vastly more capable and autonomous companies than previously possible.
