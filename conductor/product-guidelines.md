# Aidevelo Product Guidelines

These guidelines define the standards for communication, branding, and interaction within the Aidevelo ecosystem.

## Prose & Communication Style
- **Senior Professional:** All internal and external communication should be direct, concise, and high-signal.
- **High Signal-to-Noise Ratio:** Avoid conversational filler, excessive apologies, or mechanical narration. Focus on intent and technical rationale.
- **Clarity Above All:** Use precise language to describe agent behaviors, company goals, and system states.

## Brand Messaging & Voice
- **Strategic & Empowering:** Aidevelo's voice emphasizes high-level business outcomes and the transformative power of autonomous AI teams.
- **Scale through Autonomy:** Content should highlight how the platform enables organizational growth and complex orchestration through delegation.
- **The "Operating System" for AI:** Position Aidevelo as the robust, reliable foundation upon which the future of work is built.

## Visual Identity & Aesthetic
- **Optimized Modern Minimalism:** Maintain and refine the existing clean, high-contrast design.
- **Reduced Cognitive Load:** Prioritize ample white space and clear typography to keep the focus on strategic decision-making and goal tracking.
- **Intuitive Navigation:** Ensure the interface feels fast, responsive, and oriented toward high-level oversight.

## Interaction & Feedback
- **Explicit Approval Protocol:** High-impact actions, such as budget reallocations or strategic re-prioritizations, must require explicit manual confirmation from the user.
- **Transparent Activity Streams:** Provide a clear, real-time feed of agent intents and actions to ensure the user is always informed of the company's progress.
- **Contextual Workflows:** Interactions with AI agents should remain anchored to specific tasks, goals, or approvals to prevent drift into general-purpose chat.

## Documentation & Content Strategy
- **Action-Oriented Learning:** Prioritize tutorials, quick-start guides, and practical "how-to" examples that get users to their first "success" rapidly.
- **Technical Precision:** Maintain high-quality reference documentation for APIs and adapter configurations to support advanced development and customization.
- **Goal-Centric Narratives:** Frame all documentation around the core objective of building and managing successful autonomous organizations.
